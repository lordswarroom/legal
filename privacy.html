Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-18

Quick summary (human-readable):

  • If you sign in with Google, we store basic account info (UID, email, display name, photo) to recognize you.
  • We store your language preference and certain app preferences needed for app functionality.
  • Purchases/subscriptions are handled by Apple/Google; we use RevenueCat to verify premium access.
  • We may use Firebase Remote Config to control app behavior, feature settings, and update-related prompts.
  • On Android, Google Play may provide in-app update functionality when a newer version is available.
  • Premium version is intended to be ad-free.
  • We don’t sell your personal data.

1) Who controls your data

The data controller for this app is the app developer (“we”). For privacy questions:
lordswarroom@gmail.com

2) What data we collect

A) Account data (if you sign in)

Sign-in is optional unless a feature requires it. If you sign in via Google (Firebase Authentication), we may process and store:

  • Firebase UID (unique user identifier)
  • Email
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL
  • Last login time (server timestamp)
  • Language (e.g. en, hr)

B) App preferences

We store your language selection locally on your device (e.g., SharedPreferences) to remember your preferred language. We may also store limited local preference data related to app behavior, such as whether certain prompts were recently shown.

C) Purchases / subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription or in-app product:

  • Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription entitlement status (e.g. “premium active”).
  • We do not receive your full payment card details.

D) App configuration and update-related functionality

We may use Firebase Remote Config to deliver configuration values that control app behavior, feature availability, app settings, and update-related prompts without requiring a full app release.

On Android, the app may use Google Play in-app update functionality to check whether a newer version of the app is available and, if supported, prompt you to update through Google Play.

E) Analytics and diagnostics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app and to improve features and overall user experience.

  • Events such as feature usage, app interactions, and update prompts may be collected.
  • Analytics data may include device information (such as device model, OS version, and general location).
  • We do not use analytics to directly identify individual users.

We may also collect diagnostic data (such as crash logs) to improve app stability and performance.

Analytics collection is disabled by default and is only enabled after user consent where required by law (e.g. in the EEA/UK).

We may use diagnostics (such as crash reporting) in the future to improve app stability. If enabled, such tools may collect technical information like device model, OS version, and crash logs.

If additional analytics or advertising tools are introduced, we will update this policy and request consent where required.

3) Why we process data (purposes)

  • To provide sign-in and account-related features.
  • To store your settings (like language).
  • To verify premium access and prevent entitlement errors.
  • To validate daily feature limits and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • To configure app functionality and manage feature behavior.
  • To support update-related prompts and version management on Android.
  • To analyze app usage and improve features and performance.
  • To provide support if you contact us.
  • To protect the app against abuse and keep it working reliably.

4) Legal bases (GDPR-friendly)

  • Contract / service delivery: to provide app features you request (sign-in, premium access, feature configuration, update-related functionality).
  • Legitimate interests: preventing abuse, managing app reliability, and ensuring the app works securely and as intended.
  • Consent: for analytics and personalized advertising where required by law.

5) Where data is stored

Account-related data may be stored and processed using Google Firebase services (such as Authentication, Firestore, and Remote Config). Subscription status is verified via RevenueCat.

These providers may process data on servers outside your country, including the United States, under appropriate safeguards.

We use Firebase App Check and server-side validation mechanisms to help prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.

6) Sharing of data

We share data only with service providers necessary to run the app:

  • Google Firebase (such as Authentication, Firestore, Remote Config, and Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising and measurement)
  • RevenueCat (subscription entitlement verification)
  • Apple / Google (payment processing handled by the platform store)
  • Google Play (Android in-app update functionality, where applicable)

We do not sell your personal data.

Advertising

The free version of the app may display advertisements using Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads). Premium (War Pass) access is intended to provide an ad-free experience.

  • AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers (including the Advertising ID), IP address, and interaction data to serve and measure ads.
  • Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent and region.
  • In regions such as the EEA/UK, we request user consent before showing personalized ads.

We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to display consent prompts and allow users to manage their privacy choices.

If ads are added, we may use advertising partners such as Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads).

  • Advertising partners may use device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) to show ads and measure performance.
  • Where required by law (EEA/UK), we will request consent before showing personalized ads.
  • Users may receive non-personalized ads depending on region and settings.

If ads are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users may receive an in-app notice.

7) Data retention

We keep account data as long as needed to provide the service. If you stop using the app, data may remain stored unless you request deletion. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

Some local preference data stored on your device may remain until you clear the app data, uninstall the app, or reset the relevant settings.

8) Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. In the EU/EEA, you have GDPR rights including data access and deletion requests.

To request deletion or export, contact: lordswarroom@gmail.com

9) Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or under the age required in your country to consent to data processing). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

10) Security

We use reasonable safeguards and rely on trusted providers (Firebase/RevenueCat/Google Play). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version.

12) Contact
